State Wide Donation Links

Peoples City Council Freedom Fund: The Peoples City Council Freedom Fund is providing legal support, medical bills, transportation, supplies and and protective gear to protesters.

People's Budget: Los Angeles Black Lives Matter chapter is supporting organizers and fighting to adopt a "People's Budget" that reallocates police funding to vulnerable communities.

Orange County Bail Fund: The Orange County Bail Fund is helping pay bonds for protesters in Orange County Donate

NorCal Resist:NorCal Resist is helping provide bail funds for arrested protesters.

The People's Program:Peoples Oakland is a private non-profit agency established by local residents as a community, planning and organizing agency. You can donate directly to their bail fund at the above link.

DeDe McClure Memorial Community Fund:The DeDe McClure Memorial Community Fund is donating funds to free arrested protesters.
